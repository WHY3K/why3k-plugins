CountUpTimer

CountUpTimer is a free Max for Live device that starts counting the moment you launch a clip — a live 00:00:00.000 readout of exactly how long you've been playing, recording, or streaming.

Getting started

1. Drop it on a track

Drag CountUpTimer.amxd onto any regular audio track — the one holding the clips you want to time.

Note Master and Return tracks are not supported. Use a regular audio track.

2. Launch a clip

Launch a clip in Session View and the timer starts from 00:00:00.000. Launch a different clip and it restarts from zero.

3. Stop the clip

When you stop a clip, the readout freezes at the elapsed time and the digits turn gray. It holds that value until the next clip launch starts a fresh count.

The floating window

The timer also lives in an always-on-top floating window that opens automatically with your set. Hit Close to dismiss it, Open to bring it back, and drag it anywhere on screen.

A note on tempo CountUpTimer derives elapsed time from the project tempo. At a fixed tempo the readout matches the clock exactly. Tempo changes mid-song can introduce slight drift, so stick to a fixed tempo when accuracy matters.
